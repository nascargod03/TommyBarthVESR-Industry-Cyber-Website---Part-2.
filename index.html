My Core Values

Hard Work

Hard work is important to me because without working hard it is very difficult to achieve your goals.

Hard work was taught to me by my Dad through sports, school, and work, he consistently reminds me that working hard can get you to where you want to be.

Hard work is a good value to have because without it, mediocrity and laziness can quickly set in. Laziness and mediocrity cause professional and mental issues in all aspects of life.

Perseverance

Perseverance is very important because if you cannot face adversity and rebound during hard times, the chances of success are very slim.

I developed perseverance through football. In high school, my football team was very bad, only winning 1 game between my junior and senior year.

Toughing out practices and games taught me to never quit no matter how bad the score was, or how I physically felt.

I believe that without perseverance, quitting quickly becomes a habit for the worst. Without seeing things through to the end, we cannot recognize and understand our full potential.

Accountability

Accountability is important to me because if you can't be held accountable for your actions, what is the purpose of doing anything?

Growing up I was forced to be accountable for my actions and time management.

As someone who was homeschooled through 8th grade, I had to ensure my assignments were done on time, manage my time efficiently, and complete tasks to be able to enjoy extracurriculars.

All of these actions taught me Accountability. Applied to the professional world, one who is not accountable is easily left behind and cannot be trusted by their peers.
